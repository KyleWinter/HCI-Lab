N U T R I L I Z E *

The purpose of Nutrilise is to help people achieve the best health and physical and mental well-being by providing personalized nutritional advice. 'Personalization' means being able to customize nutritional advice according to their unique needs and preferences. Nutrition plays a crucial role in maintaining health, so whether it is healthy adults or adults with health concerns, they need to regularly adjust their nutritional and dietary habits. However, due to various factors, including technological constraints, busy lifestyles, food preferences, and medical conditions, many people find it difficult to meet their nutritional needs. Nutrilise aims to provide personalized recommendations based on users' personal needs and lifestyle preferences, helping them overcome these challenges.
